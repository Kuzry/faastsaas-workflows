Welcome to the FaastSaas documentation!

FaastSaas is a powerful starter kit designed to simplify the development of SaaS (Software as a Service) applications using the Next.js framework. If you build any SaaS solution, FaastSaas provides you with the foundational tools and structure you need to get started quickly and efficiently.

## Why FaastSaas?

Building a SaaS application involves many common challenges: user authentication, payment processing, and more. FaastSaas addresses these challenges by offering a pre-built, scalable architecture that integrates best practices and essential features. With FaastSaas, you can focus on what makes your product unique, rather than reinventing the wheel.

## Key Features

- **User Authentication & Authorization**: Out-of-the-box support for user management, including sign-ups, logins, and role-based access control.
- Subscription Management: Seamless integration with payment providers like Stripe for handling subscriptions, billing, and invoicing.
- Multi-Tenancy Support: Built-in capabilities to manage multiple customers, each with their isolated data and settings.
- Scalable Architecture: A robust and scalable architecture designed to grow with your business, powered by Next.js and other modern technologies.
- Customizable: Highly customizable, allowing you to tailor the starter kit to fit your specific business requirements.

## Who is FaastSaas For?

FaastSaas is perfect for developers and startups looking to accelerate their SaaS product development. Whether you're a solo developer or part of a larger team, FaastSaas provides a solid foundation that can save you weeks or even months of development time.
